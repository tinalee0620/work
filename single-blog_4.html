/ Introduction

Join me is an app that allows you to intergrate people’s time quicklyand share your spare time schedule to others in different template. It would be clear to see whether they are available or notthrough the output time form. Moreover, Join Me could hold an eventvote through hyperlink to share it in different application to avoid addingevery person in one chat room all the time. Besides, people nowdays don’t like to download too many apps, so we deside to use progressive web app to save cellphone memories and support cross-platform software.

/ Position

UI/UX Design/ Poster design/ Team leader

In this project, I designed the wireframe, mockup and poster,also made the introduction video. Being a team leader, I need to supervise the rate of the project and communicate with the professor.

/ Design conception

- Make choice quickly：You can fill the time form quickly like putting a seal on.

- Share time easily：You can send your spare time schedule toothers easily by images.

- Hold your event quickly：Use the most easily, clear and simple way to hold a date voting event.

- Save the phone memories：Use progressive web app (PWA)to avoid downloading a new app and support cross-platform software.

/ Conception and Feature

- Diverse types of templete to share time (ex. week calender/school timetable...)

- Different types of frames to correspond the topic with your date.

- Import your personal calendar to help filling your time form.

/ Mockup

Onboarding page

This four pages are the onboarding page. It describe the function of this app by using simple images and sentences.

Share your time

Three kinds of template week calendar, school timetable and month calendar allows user to share their time schedule in many ways. Students can share school timetable to discuss project and others can share month calendar to go to watch movies.

Hold an event

You can hold an eventvote through hyperlink to share it in different application to avoid addingevery person in one chat room all the time. The link will record your email and sent the result to you when the vote is finish

Different kinds of frames

Using different types of frame when sharing time can seem more beautiful, also it could let others know the purpose of this date if you choose it correctly.
